About Us

Eric Baldwin is a premier freelance guitarist performing throughout the greater Boston and New England areas. Celebrated for his sophisticated, ambient style, Eric performs as a solo artist and leads an elegant Jazz Trio featuring saxophone, guitar, and upright bass. Whether performing solo or with his trio, his music is thoughtfully tailored to enhance the atmosphere of any space, providing a rich, beautiful backdrop that never dominates the conversation.

His extensive repertoire features curated selections from the Great American Songbook, timeless Jazz Standards, and vibrant Bossa Nova classics, making him a sought-after choice for corporate events, cocktail hours, wedding ceremonies, and private celebrations. In addition to leading his own ensembles, Eric is a proud member of the renowned New Black Eagle Jazz Band, where he brings his versatile talents to both the tenor banjo and guitar.
